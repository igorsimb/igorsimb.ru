MP Monitor, a beautiful price parser for Wildberries

Monitor Wildberries prices either by hand or automatically. Ability to set a schedule for checking prices and see history of changes with a beautiful chart.

Main features include:

  • Registration and authentication
  • Unfinished orders are saved to cookies for unregistered users
  • Demo functionality with one click
  • Schedule price checking
  • Modern and stylish interface
  • Price history chart
  • Shows current average, max and min prices
